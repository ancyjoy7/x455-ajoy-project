Yi Yi (2000) ⌄

Each member of a family in Taipei asks quiet questions about life, meaning, and the way they move through everyday routines. The film follows them through weddings, business troubles, school, and first love, slowly revealing a spiritual calm under the noise of the city.

Terrorizers (1986) ⌄

An unconventional look at city life through the story of a photographer, a bored teenager, and a married couple. Their paths cross in unexpected ways, showing how random moments can change entire lives.

A Brighter Summer Day (1991) ⌄

A coming-of-age story set in 1960s Taipei. A young boy drifts between school, gang rivalries, and family pressure as Taiwan changes around him, capturing the tension between youth and the wider political world.
